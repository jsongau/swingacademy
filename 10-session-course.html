The Structure

10 sessions to build a more confident player

1 · Baseline Evaluation

Swing & throwing mechanics, athletic movement, strength, coordination, confidence, and goals.

2 · Foundation Mechanics

Stance, balance, grip, footwork, body control.

3 · Hand & Wrist Control

Grip strength, barrel control, gyroball routine, contact stability.

4 · Rotational Power

Hip rotation, core control, steel club / band, swing sequencing.

5 · Batting Cage Application

Contact rounds, line-drive rounds, hard-contact tracking under repetition.

6 · Plyometric Power

Box jumps, lateral bounds, sprint starts, explosive movement.

7 · Position-Specific

Hitter, pitcher, catcher, infielder, or outfielder plan.

8 · Tryout Preparation

Warm-up routine, skill showcase, coachability, hustle, evaluation mindset.

9 · Performance Review

Compare baseline to current performance.

10 · Final Evaluation & Next Step

Final growth report and next training recommendation.
