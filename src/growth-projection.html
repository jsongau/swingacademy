Player Growth Projection

Stats show what happened.
Discipline predicts what can happen next.

Kemuel Swing Academy combines baseball stats with training consistency — cage attendance, box jumps, sleep, nutrition, and coach notes — to forecast each player's next best step. The goal is not to guarantee success. It is to make the path clear.

Performance data

Batting average, on-base percentage, OPS, slugging, fielding percentage, total chances, and pitching stats when available.

Discipline data

Daily gyroball streak, pitching-machine sessions, batting practice, box jumps, progress logs, sleep, nutrition, and coach notes.
